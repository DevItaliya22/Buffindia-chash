Buffindia: A Vision to Transform Cigarette Waste

A Journey of Vision, Innovation, and Impact

Discover how Buffindia is redefining cigarette waste management and empowering communities across India.

Our Beginning

The Vision of Our Founder: Ketan Prajapati

The story of Buffindia began in 2015 when Mr. Ketan Prajapati, our Founder and CEO, returned to India after 12 years in Australia. Witnessing cigarette waste littering the streets and the absence of effective disposal solutions, he felt compelled to act.

Over the next 3.5 years, Mr. Prajapati committed himself to finding a solution, overcoming challenges, and innovating a sustainable approach. In October 2018, Buffindia was born, marking the start of a mission to transform cigarette waste into sustainable solutions.

Our Mission

Building a Sustainable Future, One Step at a Time

Buffindia is dedicated to:

  • Turning cigarette waste into valuable resources
  • Raising awareness about responsible disposal.
  • Empowering communities through sustainable solutions.

Our Journey

Challenges That Shaped Us

From educating the market about a new concept to proving its value, Buffindia’s journey has been one of resilience and determination.

  • 2015-2018: Research, development, and innovation
  • 2018: Officially established.
  • 2023: Launched services nationwide, serving over 1,000 clients.

Our Achievements

Making an Impact Across India

Through our efforts, Buffindia has achieved:

  • 1,000+ Clients Served across hotels, corporates, and public spaces.
  • Thousands of Cigarette Butts Upcycled into sustainable products.
  • Employment Opportunities for stay-at-home mothers, students, and unskilled laborers.
  • Inspired habitual transformations among smokers
