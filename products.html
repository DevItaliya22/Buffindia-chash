World’s First E-Commerce Store for Sustainable Decor & Gifting

Handcrafted from recycled cigarette waste, our eco-friendly products are budget-friendly and purposeful

What Sets EcoArt Apart

Sustainability Meets Craftsmanship

  • Eco-Friendly & Handcrafted: Each product is crafted with care using recycled cigarette waste fibers and natural materials.
  • Customized Branding & Packaging: Perfect for gifting, with your branding, logo, and a sustainability story included.
  • Empowering Women: Products are handcrafted by stay-at-home mothers, supporting women’s empowerment.
  • Budget-Friendly: Sustainable products that don’t compromise on affordability.

Why Choose EcoArt?

Purposeful Decor & Gifts for Every Occasion

EcoArt products are perfect for:

  • Sustainable home and office decor
  • Personalized corporate gifting with a sustainability message.
  • Celebrating special occasions with thoughtful, eco-friendly items.

The Bigger Picture

More Than Just Products

By choosing EcoArt, you’re not just purchasing decor or gifts—you’re making a difference:

  • Supporting waste management and recycling initiatives.
  • Empowering women artisans and fostering livelihood opportunities.
  • Aligning with sustainability goals while saving on costs.
