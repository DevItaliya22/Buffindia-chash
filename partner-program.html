Why Partner with Buffindia?

Make an Impact in Your Community

As a Buffindia Affiliate Partner, you’ll play a crucial role in spreading awareness about cigarette waste management while empowering your community to adopt sustainable practices. Here’s how you can help:

  • Raise Awareness: Advocate for responsible cigarette waste disposal in your network.
  • Expand Our Reach: Introduce Buffindia’s solutions to hotels, corporates, bars, restaurants, cafes, and more.
  • Promote Transformation: Inspire businesses and individuals to adopt sustainable solutions.

Affiliate Partner Benefits

Make an Impact in Your Community

  • Financial Rewards: Earn commissions for every lead you provide that converts into a client.
  • Complimentary Products: Receive free vouchers for Buffindia’s upcycled sustainable decor and gifting articles.
  • Certification of Affiliation: Official recognition of your commitment to sustainability.
  • Real-Time Updates: Stay informed about the progress of leads you provide, ensuring transparency.
  • Exclusive Training: Weekly onboarding sessions and support to help you succeed.

How It Works

Becoming a Partner Is Simple

Steps:

  • Sign Up: Express your interest by contacting us via the form below.
  • Promote: Use Buffindia’s video content and resources to raise awareness in your local area.
